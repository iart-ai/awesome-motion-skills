# Contributing

Thanks for helping grow **Awesome Motion Skills** — a curated, vendor-neutral list of AI-agent skills for motion graphics, animation, and video.

## What belongs here

A skill (or pack of skills) that teaches an AI coding agent to produce **motion graphics, animation, or video** — and that is:

- **Public and installable** — e.g. `npx skills add owner/repo` or `/plugin marketplace add owner/repo`, with at least one `SKILL.md`.
- **On-topic** — motion/animation/video. Not general design systems, static image tools, or unrelated agent skills.
- **Real** — actually works; ideally ships a way to verify its own output (render/screenshot/check).

## How to add an entry

1. Fork this repo.
2. Add one line to the most fitting section of `README.md`, in this exact form:

   ```
   - [name](https://github.com/owner/repo) — What it does, in one line. *(N skills)*
   ```

3. Keep entries **alphabetical within their section**. If no section fits, propose a new one.
4. Open a pull request. In the description, link the repo and say one sentence on what it does.

## Quality bar

- One clear sentence — what it makes and for whom. No marketing copy.
- Link to the repo (or a specific skill folder), not a landing page.
- No duplicate entries; pick the single best-fitting section.

Entries that are off-topic, dead links, or pure self-promotion without a working skill will be declined. Everything on-topic and installable is welcome — including direct competitors.
